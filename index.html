Photographer/Creator

Hey! I'm James, a Photographer Based on the south coast of the UK. I love to capture portraiture, sports and lifestyle; however, I'm happiest when shooting, regardless of the subject.

At the age of 12, I learned to snowboard, which quickly developed Into a passion I would throw myself into headfirst.

Snowboarding opened up so many opportunities for me, including travel, taking me to the most breathtaking destinations around Europe. As I progressed, I quickly fell in love with capturing the moments snowboarding presented me with. This would lead to my love of photography and videography.
