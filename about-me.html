About

After picking up snowboarding at age 12, I got the opportunity to travel to various places to practice. Being able to explore these distinct landscapes and having the privilege of enjoying totally new experiences; I knew I wanted to see the world. My passion for photography developed alongside my love of travel, and thus, its what I've been pursuing ever since.
